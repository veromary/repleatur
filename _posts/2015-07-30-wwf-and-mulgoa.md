Here I describe **Two Plans:**

1. Regular monthly Sung Mass Sunday 9th August at Wentworth Falls
2. First Communion Mass Sunday 16th August at Mulgoa

Both with Mass XI (eleven) also known as Missa Orbis Factor.

Both with Credo 1.
